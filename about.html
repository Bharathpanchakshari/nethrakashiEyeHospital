Doctor Profiles

Learn more about the skill and specialties of our core clinical team.

Dr. Meghana Panchakshari

MS (Ophthalmology) | Consultant Ophthalmologist

Specialist in cataract surgery, corneal disorders, glaucoma, and retinal diseases. Trained at Minto Ophthalmic Hospital, Bengaluru. Her MS thesis on OCT biomarkers in diabetic macular edema reflects her commitment to evidence-based ophthalmology and advanced retinal imaging techniques.

Dr. Akhilesh

MD (Ophthalmology), FVRS | Vitreoretinal & Cataract Surgeon

Highly trained specialist in vitreoretinal surgery, medical retina, and advanced cataracts. Recipient of Gold Medal in Ophthalmology from Bangalore Medical College. Extensive training at AIIMS New Delhi and Minto Ophthalmic Hospital in complex retinal disorders, diabetic retinopathy, and retinal detachments.
