Dr. Akhilesh

MD (Ophthalmology), FVRS | Vitreoretinal & Cataract Surgeon

Highly trained ophthalmologist specializing in Vitreoretinal Surgery, Medical Retina, and Cataract Surgery. He completed his MBBS from Bangalore Medical College, one of Karnataka's premier medical institutions, and was awarded the Gold Medal in Ophthalmology for outstanding academic achievement.

He pursued his MD in Ophthalmology from the All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS), New Delhi, India's premier medical institute, renowned for excellence in patient care, research, and advanced medical training. He subsequently completed a Fellowship in Vitreoretinal Surgery at the Regional Institute of Ophthalmology, Minto Ophthalmic Hospital, Bengaluru, one of Karnataka's leading tertiary eye care centers.

Dr. Akilesh has received extensive clinical and surgical training at some of India's highest-volume ophthalmic centers, gaining expertise in the diagnosis and management of complex retinal disorders, diabetic retinopathy, retinal detachments, macular diseases, uveitis, and advanced cataracts.

His experience as a Senior Resident at AIIMS New Delhi and Minto Ophthalmic Hospital provided exposure to a wide spectrum of ophthalmic diseases and advanced surgical techniques. Known for his compassionate and patient-centered approach, Dr. Akilesh combines clinical excellence with empathy to deliver personalized, evidence-based eye care. His commitment is to provide the highest standards of treatment by drawing upon the knowledge and expertise gained from training at some of India's most prestigious ophthalmic institutions.

Dr. Meghana Panchakshari

MS (Ophthalmology) | Consultant Ophthalmologist

Dr. Meghana Panchakshari is a skilled ophthalmologist with specialized training from the prestigious Minto Ophthalmic Hospital, Regional Institute of Ophthalmology, Bengaluru. She provides comprehensive eye care with expertise in cataract evaluation and surgery, corneal disorders, glaucoma, retinal diseases, neuro-ophthalmology, pediatric ophthalmology, and ocular emergencies.

With extensive clinical experience in both outpatient and inpatient settings, Dr. Meghana has managed a wide spectrum of ophthalmic conditions and has worked closely with multidisciplinary teams. She has extensive experience in cataract surgeries, pterygium surgeries, ocular trauma repairs, and intravitreal injections.

Dr. Meghana has a strong academic and research background. Her MS thesis focused on the role of OCT biomarkers in predicting visual outcomes in patients with diabetic macular edema treated with anti-VEGF therapy, reflecting her keen interest in retinal imaging and evidence-based ophthalmology. She has presented multiple scientific papers and posters at state and national conferences, including AIOC, KOSCON, and BOA, with research contributions in diabetic retinal disease, uveitis, neuro-ophthalmology, and rare ocular disorders. She has also published research in peer-reviewed medical journals.
