Comprehensive eye check up

Start your care with a full eye evaluation that checks vision, eye health, pressure, and retinal condition for patients of all ages.

  • Visual acuity and refraction testing.
  • Glaucoma screening and eye pressure measurement.
  • Retinal health evaluation and early disease detection.

This comprehensive exam provides the foundation for preventive eye care, diagnosis of refractive errors, and treatment planning for both routine and complex conditions.

Our team combines advanced diagnostics with personalized guidance to protect vision, manage eye health, and recommend the ideal follow-up or referral.
